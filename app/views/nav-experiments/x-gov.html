What this means

An Attainment 8 score of 45 means the average pupil achieved GCSE grades of around 4 to 5 across their subjects. This is a standard to strong pass.

This score is 5 points lower than the local authority average. This means pupils at this school perform below other schools locally.

The score is 5 points higher than the national average. This means pupils perform better than the national average.
