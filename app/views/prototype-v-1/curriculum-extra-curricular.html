Extra-curricular activities

Schools can offer a range of extra-curricular activities, for example:

  • community or diversity clubs - some schools may offer activities such as an LGBT+ club or a well-being club
  • creative arts
  • clubs relating to an academic subject that do not include homework or revision sessions
  • performing arts
  • sports and physical activities
  • volunteering
  • uniform groups - some schools may offer Combined Cadet Force, Guides or Scouts
  • personal and social development - some schools may offer activities such as Duke of Edinburgh Award

Current extra-curricular activities offered

Most schools offer a range of activities that are linked to, or work in addition to, the curriculum.

The range of activities can vary due to staff availability, time of year and funding availability.
